  • Analytics and Reporting:

    Integrated analytics tools to provide insights into sales performance, popular menu items, and customer preferences, aiding in data-driven decision-making.

  • Responsive Design:

    Ensured the system is fully responsive, allowing access from desktops, tablets, and mobile devices for both staff and customers.
